Merry Christmas!

I know that the best gift I have received is to have you all as my family. For that I cannot be more grateful. I have known you all for my entire life

  • but....

I realized something

There are so many parts of your lives that I don't know about. Before my life I only have small bits of stories, and unfortunately, some of those memories are not as fresh as they once were. I want to know your stories about adventures, growing up, humor, and yes romance. So I thought of a plan to fix this.

  • Here is the plan.

Let's write this stuff down!

Together we will create our own gift. This will not just be a gift to me or to you, everyone will be a part of this and everyone's stories will be added. In the end, each of us will receive a physical book containing all the stories we shared. We can all walk down memory lane together, or discover unknown parts of our family.

  • How?

Each week some will have a chance to share.

You will receive an link to a webpage that contains a writing prompt. This will be a suggested theme, topic, or idea (but feel free to ignore this). Some of these prompts will be about you, other times you may be asked to write about someone else. Simply click on the link, write, and your contribution will be saved!

  • Every week?

Don't worry

You will not be asked to write 52 stories! The number of prompts will be dispersed between the Long, Milne family, and those who may not be family but actually are. I only ask that you complete 12 of these this coming year, or one a month. You may receive more prompts, but a response to every one of them is not required! Maybe you can write a ten page story, maybe two paragraphs, there is no minimum! Even the idea of the story is enough to spark curiousity and perhaps a little in-person elaboration!
